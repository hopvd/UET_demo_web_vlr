VALORANT action across EMEA begins May 9

VALORANT action across EMEA begins May 9

Welcome to Stage 2 of the EMEA VALORANT Regional Leagues where seven Leagues are set to kick off next week, with scores of teams battling for local glory and the Champion title in the VALORANT Regional League EMEA Finals. Most VALORANT Regional Leagues will play a seven-week regular season, culminating in a one-week playoff bracket to crown a champion and league representative at the Finals.

Stage 1 saw FOKUS make the dream run from VALORANT Regional League DACH to VCT EMEA Stage 2 Challengers, thanks to their 2-0 win over BIG in the EMEA Challengers Promotion Tournament. Which VALORANT Regional League side will see their star rise in Stage 2?

Here’s everything you need to know about the VALORANT Regional League Stage 2 teams and broadcasts.
